◆ diagonalize()

void T_MESH::SymMatrix3x3::diagonalize ( double  eigen_vals[3],
double  eigen_vecs[9] 
) const

Compute eigenvalues and eigenvectors of the matrix (Jacobi method).

The calling function is responsible of verifying that the matrix is diagonalizable. Also, eigen_vals and eigen_vecs must be allocated prior to calling this method.
eigen_vals are sorted in ascending order (i.e., eigen_vals[0] is the smallest one).
eigen_vecs are sorted accordingly to the order of eigen_vals, that is, (eigen_vecs[0], eigen_vecs[1], eigen_vecs[2]) is the eigenvector corresponding to eigen_vals[0].
