48  * @class OctreeVoxelizer
49  * @brief Octree based voxelizer.
50  *
51  * This class uses an octree for voxelization. In an octree each node subdivides the space
52  * it represents into eight octants performing a kind of inherent voxelization. After subdividing
53  * the space occupied by the mesh, raycasting on the octree is performed to classify whether voxels
54  * belong to the inside or outside of the mesh. Unlike other voxelization algorithms, no
55  * additional mesh surface step is needed.
56  */
