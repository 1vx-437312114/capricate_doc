Detailed Description

Triangle of a Basic_TMesh.

This class represents a triangle of a triangulation. Each Triangle has an orientation (clockwise or counter-clockwise) due to the order in which its edges are stored in the class. When looking at the triangle so that (e1, e2, e3) are sorted counter-clockwise, the normal at the triangle points towards the observer. The field mask is useful for assigning up to 256 different states to the edge.
