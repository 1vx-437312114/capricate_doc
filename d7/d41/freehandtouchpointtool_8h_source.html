120 /**
121  * @class FreehandTouchpointTool
122  * @brief Tool for drawing arbitrary shaped touchpoints on a mesh.
123  *
124  * To draw a freehand area on the surface of the mesh, the user has
125  * to keep the left mouse button pressed down while drawing a contour line.
126  * After releasing the mouse button, the surface area of the touchpoint is
127  * created by reconstructing the mesh surface inside the marked area complemented
128  * by the contour line of the selection. Following the reconstruction, the newly
129  * created touchpoint geometry is added to the scene for rendering and to the touchpoint
130  * registry for further processing.
131  */
