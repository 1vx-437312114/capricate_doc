Detailed Description

Octree based voxelizer.

This class uses an octree for voxelization. In an octree each node subdivides the space it represents into eight octants performing a kind of inherent voxelization. After subdividing the space occupied by the mesh, raycasting on the octree is performed to classify whether voxels belong to the inside or outside of the mesh. Unlike other voxelization algorithms, no additional mesh surface step is needed.
