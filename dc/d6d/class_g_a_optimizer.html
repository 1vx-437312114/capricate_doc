Detailed Description

Optimizes the total wirelength of all connections by using a genetic algorithm.

The GAOptimizer finds an optimized solution for the total wirelength of all connections by using a genetic algorithm, a metaheuristic inspired by the process of natural selection. It relies on biologically inspired operators such as mutation, crossover and selection.

The implementation here is partly based on ideas from the blog post of Eric Stoltz for applying GA on the TSP

https://towardsdatascience.com/evolution-of-a-salesman-a-complete-genetic-algorithm-tutorial-for-python-6fe5d2b3ca35

and

Introduction to Evolutionary Computing (Second Edition) - Natural Computing Series

A. E. Eiben, J. E. Smith

Springer

ISBN: 978-3-662-44873-1
