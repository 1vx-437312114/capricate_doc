59  * @class RobustVoxelizer
60  * @brief Robust voxelizer based on raycasting and majority voting.
61  *
62  * This robust voxelizer uses raycasting to classify voxels belonging to the inside or outside of a mesh.
63  * It is based on a generalization from the Jordan curve theorem in 3D. The Jordan curve theorem asserts
64  * that every Jordan curve divides the plane into an "interior" region bounded by the curve and an "exterior"
65  * region containing all of the nearby and far away exterior points, so that every continuous path connecting
66  * a point of one region to a point of the other intersects with that loop somewhere.
