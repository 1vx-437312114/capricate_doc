43 /**
44  * @class MultiPassVoxelizer
45  * @brief Multipass rasterization based robust voxelizer.
46  *
47  * The MultiPassVoxelizer does three single pass voxelization passes based on rasterization.
48  * In each pass the rasterization is carried out along a different axis (x, y, z).
49  * The results of the three passes is combined via majority voting to a final image. If
50  * rendering artifacts occur during the rasterization step they occur most of the time only in
51  * one axis direction (often because of degenerated triangles or normals) and not in the direction
52  * of the other axes. With the multi pass / majority voting approach these artifacts vanish most
53  * of the time making the result more stable. A disadvantage of the algorithm is that it takes
54  * a bit more than three times the amount of time compared to a single pass algorithm.
55  */
