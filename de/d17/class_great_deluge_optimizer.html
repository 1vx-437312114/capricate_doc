Detailed Description

Optimizes the wirelength of a path by using the great deluge algorithm.

The GreatDelugeOptimizer finds an optimized solution for the total wirelength of all connections by using a metaheuristic similar to the hill-climbing and simulated annealing algorithms. It is inspired by the analogy that in a great deluge a person climbing a hill will try to move in any direction that does not get his / her feet wet in the hope of finding a way up as the water level rises.

[Gunter] Dueck: "New Optimization Heuristics The Great Deluge Algorithm and the Record-to-Record Travel", Journal of Computational Physics, Volume 104, Issue 1, p. 86-92, 1993

https://doi.org/10.1006/jcph.1993.1010
