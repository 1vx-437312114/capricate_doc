41 //! This class represents a triangle of a triangulation. Each Triangle has
42 //! an orientation (clockwise or counter-clockwise) due to the order in
43 //! which its edges are stored in the class. When looking at the triangle
44 //! so that (e1, e2, e3) are sorted counter-clockwise, the normal at the
45 //! triangle points towards the observer. The field mask is useful for
46 //! assigning up to 256 different states to the edge.
